Our Company

Save-X Energy is Turkish based Company with offices in Istanbul, and is mainly focused in the developing countries of the Middle East, Africa, Asia, Central and South America where the electricity networks are inadequate.

Save-X Energy Engineering Limited was founded to exploit the need for clean and green energy solutions to the growing need for provision of green off-grid power. Save-X Energy is an environmental friendly energy producer. What makes us so inventive is how we look and understand Nature and it's energy.

The sun was here from the start, it'll be there to the end, the sun powers everything that grows on earth, it’s a source of energy we can trust to pwer our world today, tomorrow and for generations to come with no harm to our planet, it's clean, safe, and infinite. It’s all about the micro inverters. Solar panels generate DC electricity and inverters turn that into AC electricity, the kind of electricity your house uses. Inverters have been large and expensive. So there was only one per home (or business). Save-X makes a micro inverter which allows the home owner (or business owner) to put an inverter on each panel. The proliferation of inverters makes solar energy far more efficient and available for every homeowner and business.

With that as our guide, we have steadily grown in the energy sector and diversified our productions profile to cover generating electricity from several sources and also save in fuel consumptions. With this in mind, Save-X Energy has developed a unique and innovative solution to the ongoing problem of securing easy access to cost-effective, clean sources of power.
